Technology

World-class modern Android stack

Production-grade architecture from day one — built for scale, security and long-term maintainability.

Language
Kotlin 2.0+
Null-safe, coroutine-native with KSP annotation processing.
UI Layer
Jetpack Compose
Declarative UI with Material 3 and Compose Animation.
Architecture
MVVM + Clean
Repository Pattern, Use Cases, strict Unidirectional Data Flow.
DI
Hilt 2.x

Product Roadmap

Building the world's wedding operating system

A four-phase journey from product launch to a global franchise-powered wedding platform — where every wedding is organized to perfection, with no surprises.

✅ Launched — Feb 2026
Initial Product Launch
Full-featured app live on Google Play — Dashboard with live countdown, Checklist, Budget Tracker, Guest Management, Vendor Directory, Inspiration Gallery & Events. Firebase Auth with complete per-user data isolation. Currently on a full free trial to build user base and market momentum.
🚀
⚡
🔨 In Progress
Marketplace & Revenue
Partner collaboration, curated vendor marketplace with native booking & payments, AI-powered vendor recommendations, premium analytics dashboard, wedding website builder, guest RSVP portal and monetization launch with subscription tiers.
🔜 Planned
Enterprise Wedding Management
End-to-end professional wedding management platform — white-label tools for agencies, professional planner accounts with full client management, SLA-backed vendor partnerships, advanced B2B reporting and enterprise SaaS licensing for the wedding planning industry.
🏢
🌍
🌟 Vision
Global Franchise & Standards
A worldwide franchise model where certified Wedzy partners organize weddings end-to-end — adhering to globally set quality standards so every couple, anywhere in the world, experiences a perfectly orchestrated wedding with absolutely no surprises. The Wedzy Standard becomes the gold mark of wedding excellence.
